HourSync

A modern way to submit eHours. Built for your future.

All the Magic

Modern Design: HourSync is designed to be modern and user-friendly. It's built on the WinUI 3 framework, so you can blame Microsoft if the apps overall design isn't to your standards.
Compatible with Accessibility Themes: HourSync is compatible with Windows' accessibility themes, so it's actually usable if you have impaired vision or need a contrast theme to differentiate items.
Drafts: We all know the pain of submitting a lengthy eHours request, only to have it lost since you were signed out. HourSync saves your progress as you go, so you can come back to it later.
Automatic Re-login: HourSync will automatically sign you back in if you're signed out, so you don't have to worry about losing your progress.
ChatGPT Sidebar: HourSync has a built-in ChatGPT sidebar when submitting eHour requests, so it becomes a breeze to write a description of your eHours.
Improved Image Viewer: HourSync has an improved image viewer, so you can see your uploaded images in a new window, and your images are not stretched to oblivion.
